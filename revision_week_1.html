Linear Algebra Aspects


  • The covaraince matrix is:
    • symmetric
    • positive semi-definite
  • Eigenvalues are non-negative
  • The covariance matrix is orthogonally diagonalizable
  • \(\mathbf{C} = \mathbf{W} \cdot \text{diag}(\lambda_1, \cdots, \lambda_d) \cdot \mathbf{W}^T = \sum \limits_{j = 1}^{d} \lambda_j \mathbf{w}_j \mathbf{w}_j^T\)
